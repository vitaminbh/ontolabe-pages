Common questions

A tile shows “——” or looks dim. Ontolabe shows a reading only when it can vouch for it. A dim value means the source has gone stale (for example, a feed that hasn’t answered recently); “——” means no reading is available yet, or your device lacks that sensor.

Sun/moon, ISS, or weather alerts seem wrong for my area. These need your location. Check that Ontolabe has location access in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

The Sound tile is blank. The sound instrument needs microphone access — audio is analyzed live on your device and never recorded. Check Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

I’m not getting alerts. Each alert is opt-in inside the app’s settings, and notifications must be allowed in Settings → Notifications → Ontolabe.
